Wealth Creation through Real Estate



When we think about how we are creating wealth through real estate, there are 3 ways that work together simultaneously:
Equity Through Amortization, Housing Appreciation, and Tax Benefits.

Use this Tool to determine the potential wealth created via Owning real estate versus Renting. Enter your scenario below and click Submit to see the Results. There are certain assumptions that are made such as having no HOA Fees for the property. The loan is a Conventional Loan and regardless of the LTV entered below, there is no Mortgage Insurance. The loan also have no lenders or sellers credits. Prepaids are calculated as 3 times monthly property tax plus monthly homeowners insurance. Same with escrows. Rent appreciation is not factored into the calculations. An Excel model is also included on github of this project that may provide more details than this website.
